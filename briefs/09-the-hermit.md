# IX - The Hermit

## Character: Shrek

### Quotes
- **Upright:** "Get out of my swamp gas"

### Tarot Meaning
The Hermit represents soul-searching, introspection, solitude, and inner guidance. Reversed: isolation, loneliness, withdrawal.
