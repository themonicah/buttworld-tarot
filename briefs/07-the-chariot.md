# VII - The Chariot

## Character: Ben-Hur

### Quotes
- **Upright:** "Ramming speed from behind"
- **Reversed:** "The race is never over till it's passed"

### Tarot Meaning
The Chariot represents willpower, victory, determination, and control. Reversed: lack of control, aggression, obstacles.
