# XVII - The Star

## Character: Tinker Bell

### Quotes
- **Upright:** "All you need is faith, trust, and a little pixie dust-er"
- **Reversed:** "Think happy toots"

### Tarot Meaning
The Star represents hope, faith, renewal, and serenity. Reversed: lack of faith, despair, discouragement.

### Composition

**Scene:** A magical Neverland night. Starry sky reflected in a still pool of water. Soft moonlight and pixie dust illuminating everything.

**Central Figure:** Tinker Bell-butt kneeling by a pool of water (classic Star pose), pouring two vessels - pixie dust into the water and into the air. Glowing with inner light.

**Tinker Bell-Butt Design:**
- Petite, delicate, glowing butt
- Signature blonde bun on top
- Green leaf dress draped over curves
- Translucent fairy wings
- Surrounded by pixie dust glow
- Serene, hopeful posture

**The Vessels:**
- Two acorn caps or flower vessels
- One pouring pixie dust into pool
- One pouring up into sky
- Creating star patterns

**Background:**
- Neverland landscape (Skull Rock distant, Lost Boys treehouse)
- Eight-pointed star (main star) and seven smaller stars
- Reflection in pool
- Flowers and plants around pool edge
- Maybe Peter Pan's shadow flying past stars

**Symbolic Elements:**
- The eight-pointed star above
- Seven smaller stars (traditional card)
- The two vessels (consciousness and unconscious)
- Bird in tree (ibis in traditional - could be a Neverland bird)
- Naked vulnerability (covered by pixie dust glow)

### Color Palette

Hopeful, magical, serene. After the storm (Tower), peace. Faith restored.
